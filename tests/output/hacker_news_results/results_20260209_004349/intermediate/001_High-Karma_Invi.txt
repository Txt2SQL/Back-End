1. Request: High-Karma Inviters. Find users with the highest karma who have actively invited other users, including a count of successful signups.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🤖 Model: gpt-4o

🧮 Query:


SELECT 
    accounts_customuser.id AS user_id, 
    accounts_customuser.username, 
    accounts_customuser.karma, 
    COUNT(accounts_invitation.id) AS successful_signups
FROM 
    accounts_customuser
JOIN 
    accounts_invitation 
ON 
    accounts_customuser.id = accounts_invitation.inviting_user_id
GROUP BY 
    accounts_customuser.id, accounts_customuser.username, accounts_customuser.karma
ORDER BY 
    accounts_customuser.karma DESC;
