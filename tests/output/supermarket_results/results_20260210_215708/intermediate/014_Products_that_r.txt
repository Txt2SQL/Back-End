1. Request: Products that represent more than 30% of their category's revenue. Identify products whose total revenue contributes to more than 30% of the total revenue of their respective category. Show product name, category, product revenue, category revenue, and percentage contribution

🧮 Query:


SELECT 
    p.product_name,
    c.category_name,
    SUM(od.quantity * od.unit_price) AS product_revenue,
    category_revenue.category_total_revenue,
    (SUM(od.quantity * od.unit_price) / category_revenue.category_total_revenue) * 100 AS percentage_contribution
FROM 
    products p
JOIN 
    categories c ON p.category_id = c.category_id
JOIN 
    order_details od ON p.product_id = od.product_id
JOIN 
    (
        SELECT 
            p.category_id,
            SUM(od.quantity * od.unit_price) AS category_total_revenue
        FROM 
            products p
        JOIN 
            order_details od ON p.product_id = od.product_id
        GROUP BY 
            p.category_id
    ) category_revenue ON p.category_id = category_revenue.category_id
GROUP BY 
    p.product_name, 
    c.category_name, 
    category_revenue.category_total_revenue
HAVING 
    (SUM(od.quantity * od.unit_price) / category_revenue.category_total_revenue) * 100 > 30;
