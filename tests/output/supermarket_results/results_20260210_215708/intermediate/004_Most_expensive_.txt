1. Request: Most expensive product in each category. For each category, show the most expensive product and its price

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🤖 Model: gpt-4o

🧮 Query:


SELECT c.category_name, p.product_name, p.price
FROM products p
JOIN categories c ON p.category_id = c.category_id
WHERE p.price = (
    SELECT MAX(p2.price)
    FROM products p2
    WHERE p2.category_id = p.category_id
);
