1. Request: Suppliers with products in multiple price segments. Identify suppliers who provide products in at least 3 different price segments: Budget (<10), Mid-range (10-50), Premium (> 50). Show supplier name and count of products in each segment

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🤖 Model: gpt-4o

🧮 Query:


SELECT 
    s.supplier_name,
    SUM(CASE WHEN p.price < 10 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS budget_segment_count,
    SUM(CASE WHEN p.price BETWEEN 10 AND 50 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS mid_range_segment_count,
    SUM(CASE WHEN p.price > 50 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS premium_segment_count
FROM 
    suppliers s
JOIN 
    products p ON s.supplier_id = p.supplier_id
GROUP BY 
    s.supplier_name
HAVING 
    COUNT(DISTINCT CASE 
        WHEN p.price < 10 THEN 'Budget'
        WHEN p.price BETWEEN 10 AND 50 THEN 'Mid-range'
        WHEN p.price > 50 THEN 'Premium'
    END) >= 3;

FROM suppliers
JOIN products ON products.supplier_id = suppliers.supplier_id
GROUP BY suppliers.supplier_id, suppliers.supplier_name
HAVING
  SUM(CASE WHEN products.price < 10 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) > 0
