Unpaid Invoices: Retrieve all invoices with a status of 'Unpaid' or 'Partial' that have passed their `due_at` date.
Account Balances: List all bank accounts for a specific company along with their `opening_balance` and `currency_code`.
Item Catalog: Show all enabled items, their SKU, and the difference between their `sale_price` and `purchase_price`.
Recent Activity Log: List the last 50 entries from `firewall_logs` including the URL visited and the user's ID.
Revenue by Category: Calculate the total amount of all invoices grouped by their category name.
Tax Summary: Show the total tax amount collected per tax type (from `invoice_item_taxes`) for the current year.
Customer Statement: for all the contacts in the `contacts` table, sum the total amount of all invoices versus the total amount of transactions (payments) linked to that contact.
Departmental Spending: Join `bills` with `categories` to see which expense categories have the highest spending in the last 6 months.
User Access Map: List all users and the specific roles and permissions assigned to them through the `user_roles` and `role_permissions` tables.
Monthly Cash Flow: Use a CTE to generate a list of the last 12 months, then join it with `transactions` to show total Income vs. total Expense per month.
Duplicate SKU Detection: Find any items across different companies that share the same SKU but have different names.
Invoice Aging Report: Use a `CASE` statement to bucket unpaid invoices into '0-30 days', '31-60 days', and '60+ days' overdue.
Top 5 Customers by Volume: Use a CTE to rank customers by the total value of `invoices` they received in the last fiscal year.
Recursive Parent Search: If the `parent_id` in `invoices` is used for revisions, write a query to find the "original" invoice for any given revision ID.
Running Bank Balance: calculate the running balance of an account after every transaction, starting from the `opening_balance`.
Top Selling Item per Category: find the most frequently sold item in every category.
Payment Velocity: Calculate the average number of days it takes for a customer to pay an invoice (difference between `invoiced_at` and the first linked `transactions.paid_at`)
Year-Over-Year Growth: compare total monthly revenue from the current month to the same month in the previous year.
Audit Trail Gap Analysis: find instances where the status of an invoice stayed in 'Pending' for more than 7 days before moving to 'Approved'.
