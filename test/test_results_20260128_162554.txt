1. Customers who spent more than average. Find customers whose total spending is above the average customer spending

codellama:13b

Query: SELECT c.customer_id,         c.first_name,         c.last_name,        SUM(o.total_amount) AS total_spending FROM customers c JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id GROUP BY c.customer_id, c.first_name, c.last_name HAVING SUM(o.total_amount) > (SELECT AVG(SUM(o2.total_amount)) FROM orders o2 GROUP BY o2.customer_id);

Outcome: 1111 (HY000): Invalid use of group function
